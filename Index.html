Storm The World (STW):

What is
Storm The World?

What is
?Storm The World

Storm The World is a hyper-casual game competition that was first launched by Storms Publishing in January 2021.

Its aim is to supercharge the growth of high-potential indie mobile game developers yet to be discovered by offering them the chance to win cash prizes and attractive publishing contracts.

Why Should You Participate?

Attractive Cash Prizes

Step 1:

During this period, you’ll be able to submit your hyper-casual mobile game by clicking the “Submit your game” button and filling in the form. More details about the submission criteria can be founhere.


Step 2:

In this round, the competition jury that comprises experts across areas such as game design, monetisation and user acquisition (UA) will select the Top 10 games based on the game’s originality, production quality as well as market fit. We will announce the Top 10 winners on the STW Discord Channel and Facebook Event Page on Tuesday, 31 August.


Step 3:

The Top 10 games that progress to the semifinal round will be awarded cash prizes. In the final round, the Top 10 will be working hand-in-hand with Storms’ UA experts to run UA testing. Depending on the test results, they will have the opportunity to unlock the first/runner-up’s prizes.
